Family Relationship Calculator

This tool allows you to build a quick family tree and then quickly calculate and see a visual representation of the relationships of your family members. Some basic relationships don't need such fanciness but when you get to second cousins once removed or third cousins twice removed it helps to see it.

Getting Started

  1. Click the edit icon (  ) on the common ancestor at the top of the tree.
    Note: This is initially valued with some sample family members to show you what you'll get, click the clear tree button in the first generation to clear out the sample members so you can start with yours.
  2. Click the add icon (  ) to add descendants to that common ancestor to fill out the tree and add generations.
  3. Once you have the family members added that you want to see relationships for, click the "map it" icon (  ) on a family member to see how everyone relates to them.
